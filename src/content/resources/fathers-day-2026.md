# When Is Father's Day 2026?

**Father's Day 2026 is Sunday, June 21, 2026.** In the US, Canada, and the UK it always lands on the **third Sunday of June** — so it moves a little each year, which is exactly why "when is Father's Day" trends every June.

If you're reading this in early June, you've got time to plan. If you're reading it on June 20th, scroll to the **no-shipping-required** section.

## Gift ideas by "how much time is left"

**2+ weeks out — order something:**
- A personalized photo book of the year
- A subscription he'll actually use (coffee, audiobooks, a hobby box)
- An experience: tickets, a class, a round of golf

**A few days out — local pickup:**
- His favorite spirit or a single-origin coffee
- A grill tool set or a good cast-iron pan
- A book in his exact niche (not a generic "dad" book)

**It's tonight — make it, don't buy it:**
- A handwritten letter (the gift dads keep)
- A "coupon book" of things you'll do for him
- A printed photo and a home-cooked breakfast

## A note that actually lands

The cards matter less than the words. A simple structure that never misses:

1. **One specific memory** ("the summer you taught me to drive the truck")
2. **One thing you learned from him**
3. **One thing you're grateful for right now**

Three sentences. That's the whole card.
